Gem Heist!

Catwoman is on a robbing spree in Gotham to raise money for a cure to covid-19! She can only carry so much in her bag! Select the gems with total weight lower or equal to her bag. Make sure you are stealing the highest possible amount you can per level.

Catwoman always puts the first gem in her personal stash (bottom right). Hit the Boost button to increase your time and score. Every unique gem gives you 20$, match 2 gems for 10 extra seconds, 3 for 30 extra seconds. Once her stash is full, she cannot add a new gem to the stash!
